Protopica helps storytellers gather, learn and build worlds with emerging technology.

We are a storytelling lab for connected worlds: part studio, part school, part community, part field research. We help artists, institutions, universities and brands turn culture into experiences people can enter.

01 / Who are we

A lab for people who know stories matter.

Everyone tells stories. Fewer people know how to translate a story into an exhibition, a course, a public ritual, a brand system, a cultural archive or a living world.

Protopica creates the room where that translation can happen. We bring together creative technologists, artists, producers, educators, institutions and companies supporting new ways of remembering and imagining.

04 / How we are doing it

A framework for moving from memory to world.

  1. Listen

    Begin with culture, archives, place, language, ritual and lived experience.

  2. Translate

    Use AI, XR, BCI, haptics, Gaussian splats, Blender and TouchDesigner as story instruments.

  3. Gather

    Bring storytellers, institutions, brands and communities into the same room.

  4. Exhibit

    Turn the work into public experiences, courses, frameworks and repeatable tools.
