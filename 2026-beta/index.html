PROTOPICA

Since the dawn of humanity, we have gathered around a fire to tell stories.

The fire is now a network

We turn stories into worlds people can enter.

Protopica is a creative and technical research lab helping creators and organizations build original worlds, preserve cultural memory and turn emerging technology into meaningful public experiences.

About

Built by people who refused to choose between art and engineering.

CO-FOUNDER / Creative Technologist

Manuel Sainsily

Creative Director from Guadeloupe, based in Montreal, working where art, technology and culture meet. With 15+ years across IBM, Immersion, Unity and Adobe, he builds AI, XR, haptics and design systems for human-centered worlds.

CO-FOUNDER / Creative Technologist

Will Selviz

Afro-Caribbean multidisciplinary designer born in Venezuela, raised in Kuwait and based in Canada. Founder of RENDRD Media, he creates 3D, VR, AR and 360 stories for clients including Nike, Prada, REVOLT, Ciroc, Bell Media and Universal Music while opening pathways for BIPOC youth.

Producer

Debi Wong

XR producer, creative director and multidisciplinary artist working across opera, creative technology and cultural programming.
