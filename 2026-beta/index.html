What is Protopica?

A place to practice storytelling for the age of AI, XR and connected audiences.

Humans have always used stories to remember, teach, gather and make sense of change. Today the fire is a screen, a headset, a classroom, a projection room, a model, a network. The need is ancient. The interfaces are new.

Protopica offers frameworks, courses, talks, exhibitions and experiments for people who want to tell stories with emerging technology without losing cultural depth, human clarity or emotional resonance.
