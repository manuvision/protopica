About

Built by people who bridge art and engineering.

CO-FOUNDER / Creative Technologist

Manuel Sainsily

Creative director from Guadeloupe, based in Montreal, working at the intersection of art, technology, and culture. Across 15+ years at IBM, Immersion, Unity, and Adobe, he has built AI, XR, haptics, and design systems. A TED speaker and guest lecturer at Harvard, MIT, Oxford, Stanford, and Columbia, he co-created MasterClass's first Generative AI course and uses emerging technology to preserve culture.

CO-FOUNDER / Creative Technologist

Will Selviz

Born in Venezuela and based in Toronto, Will is an immersive media director and experiential designer. Clio Award winner and SXSW XR director. Works across storytelling, AI, and emerging technologies for partners including Nike, Meta, NBA, Microsoft, and OpenAI. Focused on neuroscience and brain-computer interfaces, he builds inclusive experiences grounded in both design and engineering.

Producer

Debi Wong

Award-winning XR producer, creative director, and multidisciplinary artist working across opera and creative technology. Founding Artistic Director of re:Naissance Opera and co-executive producer of VIFF Signals, she has led acclaimed projects including The Dark and Live From The Underworld, performed at TED 2023, and was named to XP Land's XLIST of influential experiential designers.
